Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions and get support.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button and fill out the registration form with your details.

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse our courses, select one you're interested in, and click "Enroll Now".

Account Management

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I update my profile?

Go to your dashboard and click on "Profile" to update your information.

Courses & Learning

How do I access my enrolled courses?

Log in to your account and go to your dashboard to see all enrolled courses.

Can I download course materials?

Yes, most course materials can be downloaded for offline study.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble accessing the platform

Try clearing your browser cache or contact our support team at info@agrilearn.com.

The videos won't play

Check your internet connection and ensure your browser supports HTML5 video.

Still need help?

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.
